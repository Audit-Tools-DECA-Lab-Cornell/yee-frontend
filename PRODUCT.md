# YEE Audit Tools Platform

**Product register:** product (design serves the product)

**Users:**
- Managers: researchers and study coordinators who create projects, places, invite auditors, review results
- Auditors: fieldworkers who conduct space audits using the YEE instrument
- Admins: platform operators who manage organizations, instruments, and global data

**Product purpose:** A structured digital tool for conducting and managing Youth Enabling Environment (YEE) audits of outdoor spaces. Research-grade, trusted, professional.

**Brand personality:** Precise, trustworthy, approachable. Research tools that respect the user's intelligence.

**Anti-references:** Do not look like a generic SaaS dashboard, a startup metrics product, or a consumer wellness app.

**COPA reference:** COPA (localhost:3001) is a quality benchmark for polish and restraint. YEE keeps its own green/cream identity.

**Color identity:** Deep YEE green + warm cream surfaces. One accent color. No rainbow domain colors as background fills.

**Accessibility target:** WCAG 2.1 AA minimum; keyboard-complete for all workflows.
